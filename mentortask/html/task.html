OUR VISION


To build a community where each and every designer; whether students, aspiring architects (just like us), or already established firms/ stakeholders; collaborate, communicate and inspire each other to enhance the process of learning and creation while building a sustainable environment to live.


ARCHITECTURE


Providing Fundamental Architectural Services via Primary Consultations, Conceptual and Schematic Designs, and finally the Working Drawings.


CITY PLANNING


Analyzing and planning out Site Selection Criteria based on Block level and Neighbourhood Planning, Transportation Planning, Land-use allocation and Management via comprehensive GIS modelling.


INTERIOR DESIGN


Designing and Detailing of types of Furniture, required Design Elements, Material Selection and to overlook aesthetics of interiors based on user perception and behaviour.


TURNKEY SOLUTION


Carefully formulating a Contract based on consented design, then construct, adequately equip desired service facilities and then finally turning the project over to the client via Project Management Skills and Wage Remuneration according to rendered services


LANDSCAPE DESIGN


Analyzing the prerequisites of the site and then Detailing out spaces based on softscape and hardscape, contour profiling and modelling.


3D-VISUALIZATION


Communicate abstract and concrete ideas through visual imagery such as Elevation details, 3D - Walkthroughs, Illustrations and Animations.
